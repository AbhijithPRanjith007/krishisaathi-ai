## 1. Project Overview
KrishiSaathi AI is an AI-powered rural advisory platform designed to assist farmers and rural communities in making informed decisions related to agriculture, sustainability, and market participation.

The system provides crop recommendations, climate alerts, and market intelligence through voice and mobile interfaces.

---

## 2. Objectives

- Improve farmer productivity and income
- Provide real-time agricultural intelligence
- Enable climate and risk awareness
- Improve market access for rural producers
- Promote sustainable resource utilization

---

## 3. Target Users

- Small and medium farmers
- Rural agricultural cooperatives
- Local produce sellers
- Rural entrepreneurs

---

## 4. Functional Requirements

### 4.1 Crop Recommendation System
- Suggest crops based on soil conditions
- Analyze weather predictions
- Recommend sustainable farming techniques

---

### 4.2 Market Intelligence Module
- Provide crop price forecasting
- Suggest best time to sell produce
- Identify potential buyers and markets

---

### 4.3 Climate Risk Monitoring
- Provide weather alerts
- Predict pest outbreaks
- Suggest irrigation optimization

---

### 4.4 Voice Interaction System
- Support multiple regional languages
- Provide speech-to-text functionality
- Provide text-to-speech responses

---

### 4.5 Supply Chain Assistance
- Provide logistics recommendations
- Suggest storage and transportation solutions

---

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

- Mobile-first interface
- Support low bandwidth usage
- Offline functionality with data synchronization
- Scalable cloud deployment
- Secure data storage and communication

---

## 6. Constraints

- Internet availability in rural areas
- Availability of reliable agricultural datasets
- Regional language processing challenges

---

## 7. Success Metrics

- Increased farmer decision accuracy
- Improved crop yield and profitability
- Reduction in crop loss due to climate risks
- Adoption rate among rural users
